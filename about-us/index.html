Our Company

At ARKA GLOBAL MARINE SERVICES, we take pride in introducing you to the backbone of our shore-based ship management operations - our dedicated and expert team. Our Mission is to provide comprehensive and top-tier ship management services, and our team is at the forefront of delivering excellence

Our shore-based ship management team embodies our commitment to delivering the highest standards of service, safety, and sustainability. We are dedicated to supporting your vessel's operational success from ashore, ensuring they sail smoothly, efficiently, and in compliance with industry standards

Our vision is to be recognized as among the most respected and responsible ship managers in the world committed to safety , quality and reliability.
